# publishing toggle error

## Problem
Unpublishing a study that has enrolled groups is a silent no-op for those enrollments: no warning, no error, and enrollment status doesn't change (the reporter expects it to "kick all enrolled groups out"). Downstream of that: a residual "incomplete" status members can't clear, and a general disconnect between the study's live lessons and what an enrolled group sees across unpublish → edit → republish cycles. Since study-sync landed (2026-07), enrollments are intentionally self-owned versioned snapshots — so "unpublish kicks groups out" is now *by design not the model*, and the remaining decision is product-level: block/warn on unpublish-while-enrolled, or explain the snapshot model to the reporter. "Fixed" observably = unpublish with enrollments either warns/blocks, and no enrollment reads a perpetual "incomplete".

## Reports (verbatim)
- (2026-06-13) "If I publish a study and then enroll a group, I can unpublish it without an error or warning. Unpublishing doesn't change the enrollment status. It should kick all enrolled groups out."
- (2026-06-13) "I believe this saying 'incomplete' is a residual error from unpublishing and republishing. As a user I can't fix this."
- (2026-06-13) "There is a disconnect between the lessons in the study and the lessons the group can see once the study is published and unpublished, edited, then republished."

## Root cause
- **No-op unpublish (CF-6):** unpublish just flips the flag (`programs.ts:907-910`, `:984-992`); no cascade, no 409, and read endpoints ignore `isPublished`. Under study-sync this is now intentional — needs the product decision (block vs. kick-out vs. explain).
- **Perpetual "incomplete" (CF-3):** the three enrollment read endpoints compute `totalLessons` from the **live** `studyProgram.days` but `completedLessons` from the **snapshot** (`enrollments.ts:951-952`, `:1232-1233`) — editing after enrollment makes the denominator drift.
- **Lesson disconnect:** largely addressed by study-sync (opt-in versioned propagation) per 2026-07-19 triage.
