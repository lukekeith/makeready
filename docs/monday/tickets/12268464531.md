# publishing toggle error

## Problem
Three publish-lifecycle sub-issues, all on the **"Struggle Well"** study / **Stickane Family**
enrollment (screenshots viewed 2026-07-23):

1. **Silent no-op unpublish (CF-6).** Unpublishing a study that has enrolled groups is
   silent ‚Äî no warning, no error, and the enrollment is untouched. Screenshot 1 shows the
   iPhone study home with a **"Draft"** badge (i.e. unpublished) while the **Enrollments**
   tab still lists the enrolled group. Reporter expects unpublish to "kick all enrolled
   groups out." Under study-sync this is now *intentional* (enrollments are self-owned
   versioned snapshots) ‚Üí the remaining work is a **product decision**: block/warn on
   unpublish-while-enrolled, or explain the snapshot model.
2. **Perpetual "INCOMPLETE" the member can't clear.** Screenshot 2 (web member group
   home) shows the lesson "test lesson" (Jun 10) with an orange **INCOMPLETE** badge and
   **"No activities."** Root cause is sharper than the earlier denominator theory: a
   scheduled lesson with **zero activities** can never earn a `completedAt`, so once its
   scheduled date passes it reads `overdue ‚Üí INCOMPLETE` forever, and the member has
   nothing to tap to clear it. (The live-vs-snapshot denominator drift also exists and
   compounds this.) = CF-3. **Outstanding ‚Äî a real bug.**
3. **Lesson disconnect study ‚Üî enrolled group.** Screenshot 3 (iPhone) shows the group's
   schedule with only **DAY 1 ¬∑ test lesson**, though the reporter says the study "has
   many more lessons than this one." This is the snapshot-vs-live model; **largely
   addressed by study-sync** (opt-in versioned propagation) ‚Äî the stuck empty "test
   lesson" is legacy snapshot residue.

"Fixed" observably: (1) unpublish-while-enrolled warns/blocks (or the model is explained);
(2) no enrollment shows a perpetual INCOMPLETE ‚Äî a zero-activity lesson can't strand the
member; (3) the group's schedule reflects the study's lessons (via sync).

## Root cause
- **Sub-1 (CF-6):** unpublish flips the flag only (`programs.ts:906-908`/:975/:987); read
  and enrollment endpoints ignore `isPublished`, so nothing cascades. Intentional under
  study-sync ‚Üí product decision (block vs. warn vs. explain).
- **Sub-2 (CF-3):** a scheduled lesson with **no activities** can never earn a `completedAt`
  (completion is derived from activity progress: `enrollments.ts:771-780`), so once its
  date passes, `member-studies-island.vue:104` marks it `overdue` ‚Üí INCOMPLETE, and the
  member has nothing to complete. Compounded by `totalLessons` using live `studyProgram.days`
  (:803, :932) instead of the snapshot lesson count.
- **Sub-3:** the enrolled group's schedule is a snapshot (`lessonSchedules`); new study
  lessons reach it only via study-sync. Mostly by-design; the empty lesson is residue.

## Verification
- **Sub-1:** code-level ‚Äî `programs.ts` unpublish path has no enrollment guard (only
  `_count.enrollments` loaded for the response). Manual: publish ‚Üí enroll a group ‚Üí
  unpublish ‚Üí no warning, enrollment unchanged, study shows "Draft" with the group still listed.
- **Sub-2:** code-level ‚Äî `member-studies-island.vue:103-104` (`complete` needs `completedAt`;
  overdue when past-due & not complete) + `enrollments.ts:771-780` (completedAt only from
  activity progress). Manual: schedule a lesson with no activities in the past ‚Üí member
  group home shows INCOMPLETE with no way to clear.
- **Sub-3:** manual ‚Äî enroll, add study lessons without publishing updates ‚Üí group schedule
  keeps the old lesson set (by-design); "Publish updates" propagates.
