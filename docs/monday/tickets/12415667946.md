# member - video

## Problem
In the member web lesson player's **video step**: the UI doesn't scroll; after watching the video a "strange message" appears (the header status text, rendered under the overlapped top nav); it's unclear how to reach the next section; and the top nav is hidden. Root causes: the safe-area gap shared across four tickets (no `env(safe-area-inset-top)` anywhere despite `viewport-fit=cover`), plus the video step's single forward affordance being the header chevron (active only after 90% watched) with no in-content Continue button. "Fixed" observably: the nav/status text renders clear of browser chrome, and the step offers an explicit Continue affordance with clearer completion copy (= UX-3).
