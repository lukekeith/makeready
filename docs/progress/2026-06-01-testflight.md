This build focuses on lesson authoring and a few account and sign-in fixes. In the day/activity editor, the old help icon and "always-visible help" toggle are gone — help text now appears as inline, collapsible context on user-input activities, and you can set a custom placeholder for the input field. Read activities also have an updated in-editor preview. Outside of authoring, the Profile screen got some updates, the Home and Calendar views were tweaked, group invites and enrollment scheduling were refined, and production sign-in (OAuth) was fixed.

Please focus testing on the activity editor (verify inline/collapsible help, the custom placeholder, and read-activity preview), signing in on this build against production, and editing/saving your Profile. Also sanity-check the Home and Calendar screens, creating/sharing a group invite, and setting an enrollment schedule.
