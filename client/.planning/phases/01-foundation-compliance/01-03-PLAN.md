    Extend `layouts.app`. Content MUST include:
    - Page title: "Privacy Policy"
    - The EXACT statement: "No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes."
    - What data is collected (phone numbers for group communication)
    - How data is used (SMS notifications for group events and daily studies)
    - That info won't be shared with third parties for marketing
    - Use BEM classes: `.PrivacyPage`, `.PrivacyPage__container`, `.PrivacyPage__heading`, etc.

    Read `TWILIO_A2P_COMPLIANCE.md` for the full required language. The content must be comprehensive enough for Twilio's crawler to approve the campaign.

    Extend `layouts.app`. Content MUST include:

    - "Message and data rates may apply"
    - `<strong>STOP</strong>` — "Text **STOP** to opt out at any time"
    - `<strong>HELP</strong>` — "Text **HELP** for support"
    - Support contact info (email)
    - Use BEM classes: `.TermsPage`, `.TermsPage__container`, etc.

    **Step 5: Create sms-opt-in.blade.php (COMP-03, COMP-04)**

    - Statement: "The checkbox is not pre-checked. Members must actively check it before joining."
    - Links to /privacy and /terms
    - Use BEM classes: `.SmsOptIn`, `.SmsOptIn__demo`, `.SmsOptIn__label`

    - Import in `resources/css/app.scss` via `@use` or `@import`
